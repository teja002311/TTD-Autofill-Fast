Privacy Policy

TL;DR - Your Privacy is Sacred:

We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or share ANY of your data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

1. Introduction

TTD Fast Autofill ("the Extension") is a browser extension designed to help users quickly fill forms on the Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) website. This privacy policy explains our data practices.

2. Data Collection

We collect ZERO data. Specifically:

  • ‚ùå No personal information is collected
  • ‚ùå No browsing history is tracked
  • ‚ùå No analytics or telemetry data is gathered
  • ‚ùå No third-party services are integrated
  • ‚ùå No cookies or tracking pixels are used
  • ‚ùå No data is transmitted to any server (we don't even have a server!)

3. Data Storage

All profile data you create (names, ages, email addresses, ID numbers, etc.) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API.

What this means:

  • ‚úÖ Data never leaves your browser
  • ‚úÖ We cannot access your data
  • ‚úÖ Your data is not synced to any cloud service by the extension
  • ‚úÖ Only you have access to your saved profiles

4. Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following permissions, and here's exactly why:

storage

Required to save your profile data locally on your device. This allows profiles to persist between browser sessions.

activeTab

Required to access the current TTD webpage when you click "Fill" to autofill form fields. We only access the page when you explicitly trigger the autofill action.

host_permissions: ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in

Required to run the autofill functionality on TTD's official website. The extension only works on this domain and has no access to any other websites.

5. Data Usage

The profile data you create is used exclusively to autofill forms on the TTD website when you manually click the "Fill" button. Data is never:

  • Transmitted over the internet
  • Shared with third parties
  • Used for advertising or marketing
  • Analyzed or processed for any purpose other than form filling

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we don't have access to it.

The Extension operates entirely on your local device. There is no backend server, no cloud storage, and no data transmission.

7. Data Security

Your profile data is as secure as your Chrome browser profile. Chrome encrypts local storage data using your operating system's security features.

Best practices:

  • Keep your Chrome browser updated
  • Use a strong system password
  • Don't share your computer with untrusted users
  • Use the Extension's "Clear All Data" feature before selling/disposing of your device

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Individual Profiles: Click the delete button on any profile
  • All Data: Use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings
  • Complete Removal: Uninstalling the Extension automatically deletes all stored data

9. Import/Export Feature

The Extension allows you to export profiles as JSON files and import them later. This feature:

  • Creates a file on your local device (wherever you choose to save it)
  • Does not upload the file anywhere
  • Is entirely under your control

Note: If you share your exported JSON file with others, they will have access to your profile data. Exercise caution when sharing.

10. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect data from anyone (including children under 13) because it doesn't collect data at all.

11. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs.

12. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date below and notify users through the Chrome Web Store update notes.

13. Open Source

The Extension's source code may be made available for review. You can verify our privacy claims by inspecting the code directly.
